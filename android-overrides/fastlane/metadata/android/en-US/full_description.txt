An opinionated, beautiful app for Home Assistant.

Open it, log in, done. Your rooms and devices are already there.

ZERO CONFIG
Reads your Home Assistant setup automatically. No YAML, no cards, no templates.

EDIT IN PLACE
Rename rooms, change icons, reorder things. Changes are saved back to Home Assistant.

ACTUALLY FAST
WebSocket connection means instant feedback. Tap a light, see it change immediately.

BUILT FOR PHONES
Designed for how you actually use your smart home on the go.

PRIVACY FIRST
No accounts. Anonymous crash reporting. Your Home Assistant data stays on your network.

Works with any Home Assistant installation, including local-only setups. No public URL or cloud subscription needed.

Free and open source. Made by Twinbolt in Sweden.
